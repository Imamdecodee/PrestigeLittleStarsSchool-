Our Educational Programs

Creche & Playgroup

For our youngest stars (3 months - 2 years), we provide a safe, stimulating environment with early learning activities, nap times, and play-based development.

  • Age-appropriate learning activities
  • Safe and nurturing environment
  • Early social development

Kindergarten

Our kindergarten program (3-5 years) focuses on developing foundational skills through play, creativity, and structured learning activities.

  • Phonics and early literacy
  • Number recognition and counting
  • Social skills development

Primary School

Our primary program (Primary 1-3) provides a strong academic foundation with a blend of national curriculum and Islamic studies.

  • Core academic subjects
  • Qur'an and Arabic studies
  • Extracurricular activities
